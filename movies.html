List of IMDB top 10 movies



1.Shawshank Redemption


Dufresne, a successful banker, is arrested for the murders of his wife and her lover, and is sentenced to life imprisonment at the Shawshank prison. He becomes the most unconventional prisoner. His fellow prisoner Red observes Andy's activities in prison and is surprised that Andy never gives up hope that he will escape the prison and start a new life one day in which Red too can join.



2.The Godfather


Vito Corleone, head of a mafia family, decides to hand over his empire to his youngest son Michael. However, his decision unintentionally puts the lives of his loved ones in grave danger. Michael takes up his father's place as the head of Corleone family and finally murders every heads of rival gangs who tried to undermine the Corleones




3.The Dark Knight


After Gordon, Dent and Batman begin an assault on Gotham's organised crime, the mobs hire the Joker, a psychopathic criminal mastermind who offers to kill Batman and bring the city to its knees. As a climax of his crimes Joker tries to wage a psychological war with the entire citizens of Gotham and Batman. Will Batman and the citizens of Gotham succeed?
